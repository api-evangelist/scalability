A SIG Spotlight interview with Xing Yang, co-chair of SIG Storage, covering the group responsible for persistent data, volume management, and CSI storage interfaces. Recent wins include VolumeGroupSnapshot graduating to GA in Kubernetes v1.36 for crash-consistent snapshots of multiple PersistentVolumes, CSI Changed Block Tracking (CBT) moving to Beta in v1.36, and VolumeAttributesClass reaching GA in v1.34 for dynamic tuning of IOPS and throughput. The roadmap covers Volume Health, Mutable PV Affinity (Alpha in v1.35), and AI/ML storage trends like the Container Object Storage Interface (COSI) and data-aware scheduling.
